1. Educational Purpose Only

DermaScan is an academic project developed by students at Maharishi Markandeshwar Deemed University (MMDU), Ambala, Haryana, India. It is created purely for educational and research purposes to demonstrate the application of machine learning in dermatology.

The tool is not intended for clinical use, medical diagnosis, or treatment planning.

2. Not a Substitute for Medical Advice

The results produced by DermaScan:

  • Are generated by an AI model and may contain errors
  • Should never be used as the sole basis for any medical decision
  • Do not constitute a diagnosis, prognosis, or treatment recommendation
  • Cannot replace examination by a qualified dermatologist
If you have any concerns about your skin, please see a licensed dermatologist or visit your nearest healthcare facility.

3. Accuracy of Predictions

While our CNN model is trained on the HAM10000 dataset and achieves over 90% accuracy in controlled conditions, real-world performance may vary due to:

  • Image quality, lighting, and angle
  • Skin tone variations not well-represented in the training data
  • Conditions or presentations not included in the 7 trained classes
  • The model being used in demo mode (random predictions) without a trained model file

We make no guarantees about the accuracy or reliability of any prediction.

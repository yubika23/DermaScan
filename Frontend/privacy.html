Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Back to DermaScan
DermaScan is an educational project built by students at MMDU, Ambala. We take your privacy seriously.

1. Information We Collect

When you use DermaScan, we may collect the following information:

  • Images you upload — skin photos submitted for analysis via the Scanner section.
  • Contact form data — your name, email address, phone number, and message when you contact us.
  • Usage data — basic browser information such as browser type and device type (not personally identifiable).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Skin images are processed in real-time for disease classification and are deleted immediately after analysis. We do not store your images.
  • Contact form submissions are saved locally on our server and emailed to the project team to respond to your enquiry.
  • We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose.

3. Image Data & Medical Privacy

We understand that skin images may be sensitive. Please note:

  • Uploaded images are processed only for the purpose of classification and are never stored permanently.
  • Images are not shared with any third party.
  • We strongly recommend not uploading images that contain identifying information (e.g. face, tattoos with personal details).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Your contact form details are only accessible to the DermaScan project team (Ubika Chaudhary, Moksha, and Muskan Mehra).

5. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your data. Contact form messages are stored in a local JSON file on our server and are not publicly accessible. However, as this is a student project, we recommend not sharing highly sensitive personal information via the contact form.

6. Third-Party Services

DermaScan uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts — for typography (subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • Font Awesome — for icons (CDN-served, no personal data collected)
  • Google Maps — embedded on the contact page to show our university location

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of any contact form data you submitted
  • Ask what data we hold about you
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, email us at yubikachaudhary@gmail.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at yubikachaudhary@gmail.com or via the Contact Us form.
